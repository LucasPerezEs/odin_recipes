Choripan. Walk 2 blocks in Argentina and you'll find one"

Football stadiums, family meetings, parties. There is no barriers for a Chori.

It's not just a sandwich. It's a way of living.

Ingredients

  • Chorizo
  • Baguette

Chimichurri sauce

  • Half parsley
  • 2 tablespoons of chopped oregano
  • 4 garlic gloves crushed
  • Half cup of diced green onions
  • 1 small red chili pepper
  • 1 tablespoon of lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Chimichurri sauce Combine all ingredients in a bowl and mix
  2. Place the chorizo sausages on a hot grill until fully cooked
  3. Cut the bagguetee lenghtwise and lightly toast
  4. Place a tablespoon of chimichurri sauce on the baguette and add the chorizo
  5. Enjoy
